About Garage Zero

Welcome to Garage Zero, a trusted name in mobility solutions with nearly 11 years of expertise. Headquartered in Thika, Garage Zero specializes in delivering high-quality motorcycles, spare parts, and exceptional after-sales services.

From your first test ride to registration, delivery, and after-sales service our trained team is here to make your journey smooth and rewarding.
Join us on our mission to drive progress, deliver opportunity, and redefine mobility. Garage Zero: Your Trusted Partner on Every Journey.

Our Services

What we do @Garage Zero

⚙️
Mechanical Repairs & Diagnostics
-Engine Rebuilds & Tuning: Diagnosing engine issues, performing top-end or full engine rebuilds, and carburetors/fuel injection tuning.
-Suspension Servicing: Replacing fork seals, changing fork oil, and adjusting or rebuilding front and rear shocks.
-Transmission/Clutch Repair: Fixing shifting issues, replacing clutch plates, and adjusting clutch cables or hydraulics.
🛠️
Routine Maintenance
-Oil & Filter Change: Replacing old engine oil and the oil filter to keep internal engine components lubricated and clean.
-Chain Service: Cleaning, lubricating, and adjusting the drive chain tension for optimal power transfer.
-Brake Inspection & Flush: Checking brake pads and rotors for wear, and replacing or bleeding brake fluid.
⚡
Electrical Services
-Battery Replacement & Testing: Diagnosing electrical draws and replacing dead or failing batteries.
-Lighting & Controls: Repairing headlights, taillights, turn signals, and handlebar controls.
-Wiring & Stator/Regulator Fixes: Diagnosing charging issues and repairing or replacing faulty wires, stators, and rectifier-regulators
🔧
Specialized Services
-Customization & Modifications: Installing custom exhausts, seats, handlebars, or lighting.
-Pre-Purchase Inspections: Assessing a used motorcycle's condition and mechanical health before you buy.
-Accident Repair & Restorations: Rebuilding wrecked bikes and restoring classic or vintage motorcycle
🛞
Tires & Wheels
-Tire Replacement: Mounting and balancing new tires.
-Wheel Balancing & Truing: Ensuring wheels are perfectly balanced and aligned to prevent vibrations and uneven tire wear.
-Tire Pressure Monitoring: Inspecting tire pressure and valve health.
